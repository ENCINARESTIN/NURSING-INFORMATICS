AKLAN STATE UNIVERSITY

COLLEGE OF NURSING

Banga, Aklan, Philippines

VISION

The Aklan State University College of Nursing envisions to be the center of development in nursing education in Western Visayas Region by the year 2040.

MISSION

The Aklan State University College of Nursing shall produce quality and holistic nurses who CARE:

C-Culturally sensitive, competent, and caring nurses observant of the Standards of Professional Nursing Practice locally and globally.

A-Advocate for clients and the nursing profession, responsive to Filipino people's health needs through extension programs.

R-Resilient, environmentally-accountable, empowered, and globally competitive graduates possess transformational leadership and management for nursing development in the global community.

E-Efficient, effective, excellent, and innovative nurses who are research-oriented and technologically proficient for sustainable development.

GOALS

1. Promote critical, ethical, innovative, and transformational nursing education.

2. Produce research-oriented individuals for developing quality nursing care and sustaining resilience in communities.

3. Implement holistic, patient-centered care to various clients in various healthcare settings, recognizing their cultural diversity, and design collaboration relevant extension projects to other institutions and communities.

4. Establish intra-, inter-, and multidiciplinary linkages locally and globally in nursing care management.

OBJECTIVES

The College of Nursing will be able to produce students that are able to:

1. Develop, facilitate and extend clinical nursing interventions that will help improve health outcomes and contribute to the optimal delivery of care to individuals, families, community and society.

2. Conduct research-based extension services that promote sustainability in the community.

3. Communicate and collaborate effectively with inter-, intra-, and multidisciplinary and multi-cultural teams.

4. Design relevant independent and interdependent income-generating projects that will help in the use of potential and existing production resources in the community.

CORE VALUES

LAMPS lighting the way through uncertainties and endless possibilities:

L-Lifelong Learning:Promotes a structure of continuous learning and professional development, ensuring that our graduates are well-prepared and competent for the evolving healthcare landscape.

A-Advocacy: Empower our students and faculty to be advocates of patients and the nursing profession in upholding the quality care, integrity and social justice.

M-Mutual Respect: Fosters a culture of collaboration, diversity and dignity in the whole community.

P-Patient Centeredness: Educate Student Nurses to prioritize and emphasize holistic approach for the well-being and value the preference of each individual.

S-Service Excellence: Embody our commitment to provide the highest standard of care and education to our students, patients, and the general public. We are steadfastly committed to providing great service that is characterized by kindness, skill, and a never-ending quest for growth.
